Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Last Updated: October 30, 2025

We collect personal and analytical information to improve our services:
  • Personal details (email, name, role) when you register an account.
  • Image uploads used for mold prediction analysis.
  • Device, browser, and log data for performance monitoring.
  • Cookies and usage analytics for UI/UX improvement.

  • To analyze mold predictions using machine learning algorithms.
  • To personalize your experience and improve app functionality.
  • To contact you about updates, security alerts, or changes in policy.
  • To monitor and protect the security of our platform.

  • We use encryption for all stored and transmitted data.
  • Passwords are stored using secure one-way hashing.
  • Access to sensitive data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular vulnerability scans and penetration testing are performed.

We use cookies to:
  • Remember your login preferences.
  • Enable dark/light mode persistence.
  • Analyze user behavior to enhance performance.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may stop working properly.

You have full control over your data:
  • Request a copy of your stored data.
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Opt-out of email communications and newsletters.

To exercise your rights, email us at support@moldkit.com.

We may use third-party analytics or cloud services like TensorFlow Cloud, AWS, or Google Analytics. Each third-party provider has its own privacy policy governing their data use.

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or a site notification banner.

By using Mold Prediction Kit, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.
